about

hi i'm ioana !! i am a visual artist from romania, based in rotterdam. My art is heavily inspired by nostalgia and I love creating art works for those who still haven’t lost their child-like wonder. I specialize in storytelling and my work involves 2D animation, comic books, character designs and illustrations.

I am currently still studying animation at the Willem de Kooning Academy in Rotterdam :)
